Looking Glass Rock Trail

The Looking Glass Rock(elevation 3,970 feet) has a trail located in Brevard, North Carolina. It is a big rock and lots of people like to walk next to it and point at it and say, "Wow! I spent all that money in gas driving here, and I burnt all those calories walking here, and I spent all that time working at a job I hate so that I could make money to purchase the calories and the gas that it took to just see this stupid rock! What a great time!"

Such is life -- a compilation of agony and bliss.

If you grow ignorant of the bliss your life will become less joyful.

Thank you for stopping by and reading the philosophical ramblings of a weirdo. Now permit for me to regress to the subject of the Looking Glass Rock Trail.


Alright, now the Looking Glass Rock Trail is one hell of a trail. You can do a lot on it. Like walk, skip, get spooked, run, jump, trip, flop, scream, then get eaten to death by a bear -- in that order.

You can also trespass and camp here legally or illegally -- I honestly do not know the laws of the location as I have never even walked on this trail and I have never googled the laws that must be abided by when walking on this trail. But if you are immensly curious you can go ahead and do that yourself -- have yourself a good time!
