Clingmans Dome Trail

The Clingmans Dome Trial is the worst trail I have ever been on! Why in the hell would anyone enjoy walking around in the middle of a forest to get bit by bugs and sweat like crazy? It almost feels as if I have spent money to attend a gym of really low quality. I could have just registered for annual subscribtion to my local gym if I really wanted the heartburn. But no! I get suprise heartburn on this STUPID trail! I hate this stupid trail and the stupid people who forged the stupid damn path to the damn stupid damn trail damnit.

Next time I will not squander my time nor my money on such a stupid activity like hiking through the clingmans dome trail -- I mean could you at least inform the rodents and pests that reside here to display some courtesy to those of us who do not like being bitten or attacked?

We do not live in the stone age anymore; there is no need for the primitive act of physical violence -- I wish the animals that lived here knew that.

I honestly believe you should introduce everyone who resides in this forest to the vegan way of living -- it is a harm-free healthy diet that this country needs.


God bless you all I hope you have a good day and night today.
